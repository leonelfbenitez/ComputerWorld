About Service Center

Dear valued customer,

Thank you for choosing Computer-World Repair Service for your computer needs.

Services:
We offer a wide range of computer repair services, including virus removal, software installation and configuration, hardware replacement and upgrades, and data recovery. Our technicians are trained and experienced in working with a variety of computer brands and models.

Fees:
charge a rate of $75 per hour for our computer repair services. There may be additional charges for replacement parts or specialized software required for certain repairs. We will provide you with a detailed estimate of the costs involved before beginning any work. Also, be aware that shipping costs are not included unless stated otherwise.

Payment:
Payment for our services is due upon completion of the repair. Your total charge will be displayed in your account cart.

Data Privacy:
We take data privacy and security seriously. We will not access or copy any of your personal files or data without your explicit permission. We also use secure data wiping methods when disposing of any old or damaged hard drives.

Guarantee:
We stand behind our work and offer a 30-day guarantee on all repairs. If you experience any issues with your repaired computer within 30 days of our service, we will work to resolve the issue at no additional charge and free shipping.

Thank you for choosing Computer-World Repair Service. We look forward to serving you.

Sincerely,
Computer-World Repair Service Service Team
